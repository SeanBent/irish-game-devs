Featured Game Of The Month:

Empire of Sin:

Empire of Sin is a strtegy game developed by Romero Games and Paradox Interactive. Explore a deep criminal underworld set in 1920s Prohibition-era Chicago. Romero Games is co-founded by John Romero, a legend of the industry who developed games such as Doom, Quake, and Wolfenstein 3D. Empire of Sin was developed by Romero and his team in County Galway.

Recent Releases:

The Pale Beyond:

The Pale Beyond is an survival role-playing game that has you managing ship resources and relationships as you struggle to keep your crew alive on a freezing antarctic expedition. Developed by Bellular Studios in Belfast. Check out this trailer featuring the voice of Ian McElhinney (Game of Thrones, Star Wars: Rogue One).
